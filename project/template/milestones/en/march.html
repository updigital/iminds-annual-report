MEDIA, A SECTOR IN FULL TRANSITION

 

A dedicated innovation center

In 2012, iMinds launched MiX, an innovation center specifically aimed at the media sector. The center follows up new developments in fundamental research and turns promising innovations into feasible research projects with a limited time horizon. A program committee—with representatives from the media and the academic world—screens and assesses incoming project proposals, based on their innovative nature and their relevance for the Flemish market.

In cooperation with the media sector, two major innovation clusters have been identified for the moment and several research projects already initiated:

- Personalized media: how can media companies offer personalized content to consumers using new digital technologies? The aim is to provide content which is adapted to personal preferences, current location and device on which the content is accessed (TV, smartphone, tablet, PC, et cetera).

- Audience measurement: how can media companies measure when, where and how their content is accessed and shared by consumers? Traditional measurement methods such as viewer statistics for TV programs or circulation numbers of newspapers no longer provide an accurate picture of media consumption.

“The way we interact with media and the devices we use is changing rapidly. This makes it increasingly difficult for media companies to keep up with the needs of consumers.”
