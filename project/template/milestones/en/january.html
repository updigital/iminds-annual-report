INDIVIDUAL COMMITMENT TO A GROUP EFFORT

 

Throughout the year, iMinds stimulates activities to strengthen cooperation amongst the staff members and researchers. This begins with learning to communicate effectively and appreciate teammate strengths. More than that, it stimulates our bright minds to get together and learn more about each other. After all, no matter how talented we are as individuals, without cooperation and team spirit we will be a company in name only.

In 2012, one of our most ambitious endeavours was launched: “Bike to the Moon”.

Started on 17th January, “Bike to the Moon” stimulated researchers and central staff to cycle from home to the office – and back. The project also challenged them to participate in sporting and recreational cycling tours in order to increase the number of kilometres cycled. The target was to travel 356,400 kilometres, the distance between the Earth and the moon.

G-flux, a start-up from iMinds’ entrepreneur program iStart, developed a state-of-the-art cycling mobile app that is easy in use. The app tracks individual performance via GPS, using an easy button to “start” and “stop” your cycling trip.

The target was achieved a year later, on 22nd February, 2013.
