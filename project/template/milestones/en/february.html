DEMAND-DRIVEN INNOVATION WITH MAXIMUM IMPACT

ICT

iMinds is first and foremost a strategic research center in the field of digital technology. Each of our projects contains—to a greater or lesser extent—some digital component. This means the ICT sector is a key market segment. It includes research areas such as telecommunications, cloud computing, networks, security and identity management. iMinds is also active in numerous programs for the international development and standardization of technologies.

Media

Digital innovation offers smart media companies many opportunities to develop innovative services for new media carriers such as tablets and smartphones. At the same time, it enables them to make their content more responsive to the personal preferences of consumers. New media also open up many educational opportunities.

Health

Digital Technology will play a major role in ensuring high-quality, efficient and affordable health care for all. Our aging population is increasing the need for remote nursing and home care. New tools and medical technologies enable better, more personalized care and will allow governments to focus more on preventive health care.

Energy

The production of green energy creates new challenges for existing energy distribution networks, since production and consumption are much more difficult to reconcile. Smart grids are capable to respond more flexibly to fluctuating demands for energy. Smart homes and electric cars continue to offer a vast potential to preserve natural resources.

Smart Cities

Worldwide, an increasing number of people are living in urban environments. Cities will therefore have to undergo a major transformation if they are to continue to ensure mobility, housing and public services for the growing influx of people. An efficient infrastructure, supported by innovative digital technology applications, will play a crucial role.

Manufacturing

To remain competitive, companies must become high tech environments where flexibility, quality and efficiency are central. Meanwhile, a new trend is emerging for customized, highly personalized products manufactured close to local markets. In addition, new technologies such as 3D printing offer unprecedented potential.

“Goal-oriented and smart innovation is the way to create jobs and improve the competitive position of Flemish companies. With focused investments, we will be able to help companies develop and commercialize their innovations much faster. Only in this way can we create new jobs and secure our existing jobs.”
