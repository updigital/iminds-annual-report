GROWING THE IMINDS ORGANIZATION

 

BRAND AWARENESS

IBBT became iMinds

iMinds started in 2004 as IBBT (Institute for Broadband Technology), a name that has long ceased to cover the breadth of our activities. Our new name reflects perfectly what we stand for today. We are an organization in which talented people— researchers as well as staff—are central and collaborate on digital technology solutions that will have a lasting and positive impact on economy and society.

INTERNATIONALIZATION

Leading at a global level

Digital innovation is increasingly taking place within an international context. In 2012, iMinds strengthened its presence on the international stage, including a key role in European bodies such as the Connect Advisory Forum of the European Commission and the KIC ICT Labs. Our community of researchers is increasingly both varied and international with 55 nationalities currently present. We have also signed partnerships with key universities and research institutes around the world. In the coming years, iMinds will further strengthen its international presence, without of course losing sight of its solid grounding in Flanders.

PROFESSIONALIZATION

A strong and mature organization

To play on the international stage as an innovation center, we must strive for operational excellence and innovation in every field. That includes everything from the execution of research projects and the start-up of new businesses to the organization of events. The increasing number of (research) projects in which we are involved requires a strong management and a professional approach at every level. We laid the foundations for this with a new organizational structure in 2012. The new framework agreements with five Flemish universities also fit into this plan.
