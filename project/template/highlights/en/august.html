iMinds at the ENoLL Summer School 2012

 

Between the 20th and 23rd of August 2012, iMinds took part in the European Network of Living Labs Summer School in Helsinki - Finland. The event raised a major question: what would happen if everybody could be an innovator?

Koen Vervoort, Responsible for the Panel Management of iMinds Living Labs, shares his experience:

 

“Personally I don’t think the question is if everybody COULD BE an innovator but the question is how we can make people aware of the fact that everyone IS an innovator. We all do things in life during which we are always innovating, we’re just not quite aware of it. I was a little bit surprised though by the fact that so many initiatives are struggling to connect with Living Lab-users. It looks like many ENoLL Living Labs don’t have or obtain the resources they need to create a good structure and relationships with their end-users. Quite a few didn’t even know how to start with that one. So let us reach out by sharing knowledge and the experience we gathered over the last few years within iMinds Living Labs, without making the assumption of knowing it all. From my point of view, there should be so much more interaction between cross border Living Labs, in order to find a sounding board to our own difficulties, creating a bigger box for thinking outside the box.”
