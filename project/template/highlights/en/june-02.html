iStart TagTagCity Rewarded!

 

On June 26, TagTagCity was elected European start-up of the year 2012 at the European ICT Award. The award is granted every year during the ICT Spring in Luxemburg.

TagTagCity was selected on the 2012 call for iStart projects. Their business idea is built around the question of how one can smoothly bring multimedia content about a certain geographical location to the Smartphone user.

 

 

“We created a platform through which any shop owner or cultural organisation can create a standardised mobile website in a way that is cost efficient. The sites are made accessible by means of a sticker with a QR code, with a shortened URL, and a built in NFC chip.”
