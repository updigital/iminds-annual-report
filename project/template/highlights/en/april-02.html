Ghentrepreneur: students become entrepreneurs

 

In 2012 iMinds stood at the cradle of Student Ghentrepreneur, with a kick-off event that happened on April 16th. The aim is to encourage student-entrepreneurs to develop their ideas into a successful company. During the academic year, they get the freedom to work on their project. They also get the necessary funding to take their initial steps toward entrepreneurship. With the success of the pilot project in Ghent, the program has been extended to students in Antwerp, Brussels and Leuven.
