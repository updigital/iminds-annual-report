iStart.light program for student entrepreneurs: call 2012

 

Flemish future entrepreneurs should get the change to start their business. That's why iMinds grants funding to student entrepreneurs via the iStart.light program - to motivate students not only to work on their project, but build a business around it.

iStart.light:

• is specially designed for students that are young entrepreneurs

• provides them with financial support

• provides a consistent guidance
