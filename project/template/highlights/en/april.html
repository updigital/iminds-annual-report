SUPPORT

From coaching to infrastructure

Making the transition from the academic world or a regular job to becoming an entrepreneur is not always obvious or easy. iMinds assists innovative start-ups every step of the way. Aspiring entrepreneurs can register for iBoot, a training camp where they transform their ideas into concrete and achievable projects in the span of just one week. iMinds also provides iCubes and Start-up Garage, a wide range of ready-to-move office space and additional services for start-ups.

CONNECT

Ecosystem of entrepreneurs, know-how and capital

Over the years, iMinds has built an ecosystem of entrepreneurs, financial organizations, industrial partners and research departments. During workshops, special events and other networking opportunities, innovative minds can meet, get inspired, share experiences and find specialized service providers and investors.

FINANCE

From start-up to international success

Finding funding for a new business is not easy, especially in the current economic climate. iMinds helps start-up technology companies to find appropriate investors through enterprise programs like “Go Global.” The iStart program focuses on start-ups arising from iMinds research projects, while it is also open to Flemish companies looking for seeding capital. iMinds also supports these companies in the search for follow-up financing through, for example, the Spin-Off Financing Instrument (SOFi) - which secured investments for four iMinds spin-offs in 2012.

“iMinds is a great environment to have your first experience and learn, because we build a strong feeling of trust that helps us make the transition from the academic world to the entrepreneurial world.”
