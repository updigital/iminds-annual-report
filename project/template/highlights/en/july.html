MiX Launches its first call for projects

Wanted: innovative ideas!

 

On 10th July, iMinds’ Media Innovation Centre launched its first call for projects in tune with media parameters. What kind of projects was MIX aspiring at precisely on its first call? We asked director Martijn Bal.

 

Martijn Bal, Director iMinds Media: “For our first call, in July 2012, we had two directions to follow. During various conversations with the media, two outspoken tendencies came forward. On the one hand, there was a strong demand from the media partners for figures on media consumption. Who’s reading what? When and who is watching which program? Which devices does the consumer have at reach when watching, listening and reading? Not only figures about the consumer, but also figures about the content are relevant.

On the other hand, the media partners wished to evolve towards a more personalised offer. They wanted to offer content that was tailor-made for the consumer. By interacting with the consumer and gathering information about his/her watching, reading and listening behaviour, they could then adapt their offer to comply with these expectations. This evolution also smooths the path towards targeted advertising. Less commercial media partners could get to know their target group better and make their brands stronger thanks to this interaction with the consumer.”

The MiX call of 2012 resulted in the selection and start-up of six projects.
