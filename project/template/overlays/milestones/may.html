A UNIQUE RESEARCH MODEL

ICON stands for Interdisciplinary Cooperative Research and is a rather unique research model. Companies, governments or other organizations that have identified specific needs with their users, can start a research project in collaboration with iMinds to address these needs. iMinds—along with its associated university research departments—then goes looking for other partners that may be interested in the research and can help carry it out. 

Since the start of iMinds in 2004, no less than 81 ICON projects have been successfully completed. Numerous innovations—including Scotty.be, Bednet, highway track control and the eHealth platform of the Flemish government—are based on research results from ICON projects.

The 2012 call brought a simplified procedure: first, the project consortia participate with an extended abstract and related pitch to an expert panel. Only after this early approval, a full proposal is written for final selection. This two-step approach ensures low time investment up-front for preparation of the proposal.

The simplicity of the new method is useful in many ways: 

1) to perform a first assessment of business ideas coming from researchers, 

2) to create a technology transfer-oriented mindset and 

3) to prepare the researchers to build short and concise value proposition.
