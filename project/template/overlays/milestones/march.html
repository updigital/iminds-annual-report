INDIVIDUAL COMMITMENT TO A GROUP EFFORT



Throughout the year, iMinds stimulates activities to strengthen cooperation amongst the team members. This begins with learning to communicate effectively and appreciate teammate strengths. More than that, it stimulates our bright minds to get together and learn more about each other. After all, no matter how talented we are as individuals, without cooperation and team spirit we will be a company in name only.

In 2012, one of the institute’s most ambitious endeavours kicked-off: Bike to the Moon.

Started on January 17th, “Bike to the Moon” stimulated researchers and central staff to take the bike from home to the office – and back. The project also challenged them to participate in sportive and recreative biking tours in order to increase the number of kilometers biked. The goal was to travel 356,400 kilometers, the distance between the Earth and the moon.

G-flux, a start-up from iMinds’s entrepreneur program iStart developed a state-of-the-art cycling mobile app that is easy in use. The app tracks individual performance via GPS, using an easy button to "start" and "stop" your cycling trip. Bike to the Moon proved to be very hard but great fun and an incredible teambuilding event.
