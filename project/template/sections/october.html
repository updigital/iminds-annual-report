STIMULATING INNOVATION AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP

iMinds encourages researchers, entrepreneurs and businesses by creating ideal conditions for successful innovation. We pay specific attention to the needs of start-ups and SMEs. One of the main activities that iMinds carries out in this domain is iBoot, a series of workshops to help both iMinds' researchers and external idea owners discover the potential of their idea through hands-on training and coaching. The selected start-ups for iBoot 2012 were presented on 23rd April.

INDIVIDUAL COMMITMENT TO A GROUP EFFORT

Throughout the year, iMinds stimulates activities to strengthen cooperation amongst staff members and researchers. In 2012, one of our most ambitious endeavours was launched on 17th January: a 356,400 kilometres bicycle ride to the moon.
