COLLABORATING FOR THE CARE OF THE FUTURE

Over the years, iMinds has launched more than 25 research projects in the health care sector. Today iMinds is an important partner with the Flemish government in the realization of the Flanders Care program, which is part of the Vlaanderen in Actie (ViA) action plan. In September 2012, Flemish Minister of Innovation Ingrid Lieten launched an open innovation lab (Zorginnovatieruimte Vlaanderen), which iMinds is coordinating.
